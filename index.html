About Benny's Ushers

What we do?

Benny's Ushers is an Ushering Brand based in Nigeria known for Event Management and organization. Our Ushers and Usherettes are devoted to the maximum Satisfaction of your Guests.
Other services we render Includes:

  • Event venue decoration
  • Catering services
  • Event coverage and photography
  • Event planning
  • MC/Entertainer

Event Planning and Decoration

Catering
